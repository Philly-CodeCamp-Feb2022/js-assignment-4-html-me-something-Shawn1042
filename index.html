Hunter x Hunter!


I used to love watching anime as a kid, then video games took over my life, and wellll I took a little hiatus from watching anime, try 10 years. I didnt even know Dragon Ball Super was a thing until 2019, yeah... I know. Ever since I branched out of watching just Dragon Ball in late 2019 after I finished Super, I watched Hunter x Hunter!Hunter x Hunter is my 2nd favorite anime of all time, behind Attack on Titan(goat)!
What I loved about Hunter x Hunter; the animation, characters, the story-telling, the arcs, and the character development in Gon, Killua, Kurapika, and even Leorio! I also loved the backgrounds of every character, especially Kurapika. Hunter x Hunter had it all, dispense, happy, cliffhanging, detective, exciting and sad moments, and most of all, a great story.
This section isn't just about telling you that I love Hunter x Hunter, It's about the different stories and multiple factors as to why I like anime in general, Hunter x Hunter was a great example, I know :)

Attack on Titan


Attack on Titan is my favorite anime! I will always recommend that everyone and anyone, watch this anime! Why do I love Attack on Titan so much you might ask? Well, the music is amazing, the animation is great, the world building is extremely unique, the characters are incredibly interesting, and I could go on and on for days. What I love the most about Attack on Titan is that every character is important and has a purpose, I also value the suspense and cliffhangers at the end of each episode!
The MC(main character) is also another reason why I love Attack on Titan, Eren Yeager's character development has been one of my favorites. He's also not a basic anime character either, he explains his ideals and actions and he is also incredibly intelligent. Eren was able to manipulate the entire world, especiallypeople closets to him. I also thoroughly enjoyed every single second of every single season, everything had a meaning behind it, there was always something planned, and always adversity! I recommend you go watch it! 10/10!

My Hero Academia!

My Hero Academia is my favorite new gen anime! This anime is about a boy named Izuku Midoriya, who lives in a world where everyone has powers(they call it quirks), but he is born with none! I mlove this anime because is depicts a new super hero new gen! The world building is fsily basic, but plays a huge part in the story, the characters are very interesting(especiallythe main villain named Shigaraki), the music is very very good, the arcs are incredible,
lots of funny moments, the main villain has turned into my favorite villain of all time, so far. The character development is also very impressive, THE CHARACTER DESIGNS ARE EXTREMELY GREAT, but the biggest thing that gravitates me to My Hero Academia are the villains! The villans in My Hero Academia are S tier(top tier), in my opinion of course. Each villain has a unique and dark background, utlizied in different ways,and each have a very unique power(quirk).
I also thoroughly enjoy the story as a whole, the boy who was born without any powers, climbing his way to the top, to become the number 1 and most reliable hero in the world! 9.5/10, I recommend!

Death Note

Death Note is oen of my favorite animes, and one of the greatest animes of all time because of everything thats included into the anime! I personally, am in love with detective investigations, so Death Note was perfect for me. Death Note is about a boy who finds a very peculiar book on the ground, in this book he has the ability to kill anyone he wants by simply, writing their name down inside the book and thinking of their face as well. The boys name is Light Yagami, formally known as Kira. Light started killing all criminals in Japan and around the world to reduce the amount of crime and violence in the world, but an extremely smart detective who goes by the name "L", thought otherwise. So now it's a battle between a high school genius and a detective whos intelligence is equivalent to many investigation agencies!
Light has a god complexion as well which is interesting, I highly recommend you watch this series to learn more, a God vs a born genius. Who wins?
